About FinTrack

We're on a mission to empower investors with intelligent portfolio tracking solutions

Our Story

FinTrack was born out of frustration. In 2019, three finance professionals struggled to find a user-friendly yet powerful tool to track their diverse investment portfolios. Excel spreadsheets were cumbersome, and existing solutions lacked the analytical depth they desired.

They envisioned a platform that combined sophisticated investment analytics with an intuitive user interface, accessible to both novice investors and financial experts alike. This vision became FinTrack.

Today, FinTrack serves over 10,000 investors worldwide, from individual retail investors to professional financial advisors. We're committed to continuous innovation and helping our users make data-driven investment decisions.
