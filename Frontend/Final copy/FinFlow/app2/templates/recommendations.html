General Financial Tips

Emergency Fund

Aim to save 3-6 months of expenses in an easily accessible account for unexpected situations.

This provides financial security and peace of mind.

Debt Management

Focus on paying high-interest debt first while maintaining minimum payments on other debts.

This strategy can save you money in interest payments over time.

Investing

Consider starting with retirement accounts and diversifying your investments.

Even small regular contributions can grow significantly over time.
